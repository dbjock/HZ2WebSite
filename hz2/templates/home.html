Welcome

Here is a simple/quick way to find out what resources are needed to upgrade your Horizion Zero Dawn:Forbidden West gear.

Why

I have put in over 150 hours of Forbidden West, and I have loved every minute of it. When new game plus came out I was all of going thru it again ultra hard though I knew I needed to get the ng+ legendary weapons to hopefully succeed. That put me on a mission to figure out what resources and how many to get them.

That was a long and hard, had to go to many sites, and play long hours collecting that information. I wanted to help out the other players starting (or replaying) in planning out the cost in resources to obtain the items they want. So I consolidated the data I found, along with new info as I played to create a database and this site to get to the data.

Credits

A shout out to Italicman who's reddit post provided me his Horizon Forbidden West - Item Upgrades & Stats Tool spreadsheet which provided me a majority of the pre-newgame data for me to start with.

Also game8.com which was used to fill in some blank info.

Help

There is lots of data, and game updates, so I expected some corrections will be needed. Please help me help you with good data.

If you find something that needs to be corrected let me know, by sending an email to junkyardjojo at gmail dot com, with what info is incorrect, and what it should be corrected to. That will be a big help.
